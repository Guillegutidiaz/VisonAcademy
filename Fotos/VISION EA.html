Foundations of Algorithmic Trading

This course is designed to build a solid foundation in trading and finance before moving into automation. You will learn how markets actually work, how brokers execute trades and why concepts like spreads, margin, leverage and risk management matter in real trading environments.

The focus is not on shortcuts or indicators, but on understanding the mechanics behind every automated system. By the end of the course, you will clearly understand what changes when moving from demo to real capital.

  • Market basics and trading mechanics
  • Orders, execution, spreads and slippage
  • Risk management and position sizing
  • From manual logic to automated rules
