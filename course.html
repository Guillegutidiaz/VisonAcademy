FOUNDATION PROGRAM

Foundations of Algorithmic Trading

Understand how systematic trading really works before deploying real capital.

This course is designed for traders who want to move beyond indicators and intuition, and gain a structured understanding of how algorithmic trading systems operate in real markets.

We focus on execution, risk behavior and system logic — the elements that determine whether a strategy survives outside backtests.

  • How markets, brokers and MetaTrader actually work behind the interface
  • Orders, spreads, slippage and execution in real conditions
  • Market structure: trends, ranges and key price levels
  • From discretionary rules to systematic strategies
  • Building indicators and Expert Advisors in MQL5
  • Strategy testing, optimization and robustness evaluation
  • Why most EAs fail in real accounts
  • Intro to Python for analysis and strategy validation
  • Infrastructure considerations: VPS and execution stability
